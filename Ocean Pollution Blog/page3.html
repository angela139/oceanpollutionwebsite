How

Can You Help Solve This Problem?

There are
many ways to help with ocean conservancy.


  • People can use less plastic products to reduce the amount of plastics that end up in the ocean. Alternatives for plastic products include using a reusable water bottle, putting food in metal containers, and bringing a reusable cloth bag to store groceries when shopping.


  • Another way to help is taking care of trash in local beaches by cleaning up after yourself and others. To find information on beach cleanups, search online or learn about the Surfrider Foundation by clicking on the "Programs" tab above.


  • To minimize climate change that affects oceans, be more mindful of your energy usage. Ways to reduce your energy consumption include taking the bus instead of driving, using fluorscent light bulbs, and buying energy-efficient appliances like something solar-powered.


  • Bringing more attention to ocean conservancy is also important, so another way to help is to talk to local government officials about your concerns or volunteering at places who support ocean conservation.


  • To find more ways to help your community with saving the ocean, visit the "Programs" tab for organizations that benefit the cause.
