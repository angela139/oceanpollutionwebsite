Environmental Defense Fund

The Environmental Defense Fund is a nonprofit organization that try to solve challenges in the current environment. The organization is made up of scientists, economists, and other professionals who work with other businesses and the government to achieve their environmental goals. Some of their accomplishments include reducing overfishing in the United States through an implemented system, persuading companies to be more environmentally friendly, and taking action to reduce gas emissions that affect the climate.

The Nature Conservancy

The Nature Conservancy is a nonprofit organization that has four goals: tackling climate change, protecting the land and water, providing food and water sustainably, and building healthy cities. To tackle climate change, the organization plants trees which can decrease carbon dioxide levels and works with governments to create solutions that can benefit the environment. They secure food and water by managing the fishing industry through safer fishing practices, working with farmers to protect forests, and creating water funds.

Ocean Conservancy

The Ocean Conservancy is a organization that tries to find solutions to ocean pollution and hopes to create a healthy ocean for our ecosystem. The organization was started by Bill Kardash who was passionate about whales and their protection. At first, the program only focused on whales, sea turtles, and seals, but branched off to protecting their entire habitat. They have several programs which include protecting the Artic, restoring the Gulf of Mexico, and working with the government to get funding for ocean conservation.

Surfrider Foundation

The Surfrider Foundation in San Francisco is a nonprofit organization with volunteers that focus on organizing beach cleanups, gatherings, and events that educate others about protecting oceans/beaches. The organization was founded in 1984 by surfers and now has 500,000 members around the world. They have scheduled cleanups in SF usually for the first, third, and last Sunday each month.
